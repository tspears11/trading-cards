Giraffe

Giraffes give birth standing meaning the calf is in for a 5 foot drop (ouch!).

  • Scientific Name: genus Giraffa
  • Average Height: Females may reach 14ft while males can exceed 18ft
  • Average Lifespan: Up to 26 years in the wild and longer in captivity.
  • Habitat: Sub-Saharan grasslands, savannas, and woodlands

Giraffes are the worlds tallest land mammal. They are peaceful and stay near other herd animals for safety in numbers. Africa is the only continent on which giraffes are found in the wild.
